Resources

Tor

Routes traffic through the Tor network, preventing tracking and protecting against surveillance and censorship to protect your privacy and anonymity.

I2P

A fully encrypted peer-to-peer overlay network that routes traffic inside its network, providing anonymous communication and obfuscates user data.

Pastebin

Online platform for sharing text-based content. Monitored for leaked credentials, documents, stolen data, and other potentially malicious threats.

GitHub

Online platform for hosting and collaborating on code, tracking changes, managing issues, and sharing projects across open-source and teams.

Discord Webhooks

Lets apps send automated messages or alerts into channels, enabling integrations, notifications, and botless updates in real time.
